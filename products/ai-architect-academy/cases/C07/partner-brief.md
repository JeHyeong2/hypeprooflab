## 4. How It Works

### 4.1 The 90-Minute Experience

```
[0:00]  AI Demo (WOW moment — AI creates a game in 30 seconds)
[0:05]  Family fills out Game Design Card (name, character, rules)
[0:10]  "Good instructions vs. bad instructions" mini-game
[0:20]  BUILD: Child directs AI to create their game (core experience)
[1:00]  Polish and save — game gets a permanent URL
[1:10]  Mini showcase — each family demos their game on big screen
[1:20]  Photo moment + permanent game link + workshop flyer
[1:25]  Reset for next session
```

### 4.2 What Families Take Home

1. **A playable game** with a permanent URL — shareable with friends and family
2. **A completion sticker** — instant gratification for children
3. **A photo** of the family with their game on the big screen
4. **A story** — "We made a video game together at [Your Park] using AI"

This is the kind of experience that generates **organic social media posts** from parents.

## 6. Operational Compatibility

| Park Concern | Our Solution |
