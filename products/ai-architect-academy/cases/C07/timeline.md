# C07 — Theme Park Popup Workshop: Timeline

---

## Overview

Theme park procurement is inherently slow (6-12 months from first contact to contract). This timeline accounts for the lengthy BD cycle, space design/buildout, staff training, and seasonal launch optimization.

**Target: Pilot launch Summer 2027 (peak season)**

- Satisfaction: 4.5+/5.0
- No critical operational failures (equipment, safety)

## Parallel Track: KidZania (Fast-Track Alternative)

KidZania has shorter procurement cycles (3-6 months vs. 6-12 months for Everland/Lotte World) because:
- Smaller organization, faster decision-making
- Education content is their core business (vs. supplementary for theme parks)
- They actively seek new "job experience" content partners

| Phase | KidZania Timeline | Standard Park Timeline |
|-------|------------------|----------------------|
| First contact to LOI | 2-3 months | 4-6 months |
| Contract to launch | 2-3 months | 4-6 months |
| **Total** | **4-6 months** | **8-12 months** |

**Recommendation:** Pursue KidZania in parallel as a faster proof-of-concept while Everland/Lotte World procurement proceeds.
