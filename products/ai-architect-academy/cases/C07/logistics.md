```
SESSION END (T+0)
│
├─ T+0 to T+3: TEARDOWN (Staff A + B)
│   ├─ Collect headset from instructor
│   ├─ Collect Game Design Cards (recycle bin)
│   ├─ Clear sticker/card debris from desks
│   └─ Return laptops to template home screen (Ctrl+W all tabs, open template page)
│
├─ T+3 to T+5: CLEAN (Staff C)
│   ├─ Wipe all desk surfaces with cleaning wipe
│   ├─ Straighten chairs
│   └─ Check floor for dropped items
│
├─ T+5 to T+10: SETUP (Staff A + B)
│   ├─ Place fresh Game Design Cards on each desk
│   ├─ Place fresh role cards on each desk
│   ├─ Place sticker sheet on each desk
│   ├─ Verify all 10 laptops display template selection screen

│
├─ T+5 to T+10: QUEUE-IN (Staff C, simultaneous with Setup)
```

## 4. Weather Contingency

### 4.1 Indoor-Only Policy

**The theme park popup MUST operate in an indoor, climate-controlled zone.** This is non-negotiable in the contract because:
- Laptops and electronics cannot tolerate rain, humidity, or direct sunlight
- Extreme heat (35C+) or cold (0C-) affects both equipment and participant comfort
- Screen glare in outdoor environments makes the experience unworkable

### 4.2 Contingency for Semi-Outdoor Zones

If the only available zone is semi-outdoor (covered but open-sided):

| Weather Event | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| Light rain | Operate if covered and electronics are protected. Monitor wind direction. |
| Heavy rain / thunderstorm | Cancel session. Refund or reschedule walk-ins. Pre-booked families offered next available slot. |
| Extreme heat (35C+) | Operate only with functioning AC or fans. Cancel if zone temperature exceeds 30C. |
| Extreme cold (below 5C) | Cancel. Laptop batteries drain fast, participants uncomfortable. |

### 4.3 Cancellation Protocol

| Trigger | Decision Time | Action |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Weather forecast: storm warning | D-1 evening | Cancel pre-booked sessions, update park app listing |
| Sudden weather change | 30 min before session | Queue staff announces cancellation, offers future session QR code |
| Mid-session weather event | Instructor judgment | Complete if possible; evacuate per park emergency protocol if unsafe |

**Annual weather cancellation budget:** ~10 sessions/year (built into annual projection as downside scenario)

## 6. Participant Communication Plan

Theme park popup sessions involve two distinct booking modes: **pre-booked** (40%) and **walk-in** (60%). Communication plans differ by mode.

### 6.1 Pre-Booked Participants

| Touchpoint | Timing | Channel | Content |
|------------|--------|---------|---------|
| **Booking Confirmation** | Immediately after booking | Email + KakaoTalk | Booking details, session time, zone location within park, what to expect ("Your child will make a real game in 90 minutes — no coding required") |
| **D-7 Reminder** | 7 days before session | Email + KakaoTalk | Confirm booking, "No preparation needed — just bring yourselves and a sense of adventure". Park parking/transit tips. |
| **D-1 Reminder** | Day before session | KakaoTalk push | Session time, zone entrance point with map screenshot, weather reminder (indoor zone — no weather issues). "Can't make it? Cancel by 10PM tonight for no charge." |
| **D-Day: 30min Before** | 30 min before session start | KakaoTalk push | "Your session starts in 30 minutes! Head to [Zone Name] near [Landmark]." QR code for zone check-in. |
| **D+1 Follow-Up** | Day after session | Email | Child's game URL (permanent link), "Your game was played X times!" (if tracking enabled), full workshop invite with ₩30,000 discount QR, NPS survey (3 questions). |
| **D+7 Nurture** | 7 days after session | Email | "Here's how [Park] families used AI this week — and what your child can do next." Full workshop dates and registration link. |
| **D+14 Last Call** | 14 days after session | Email | Discount code expiry reminder. "Offer valid until [date]." |

### 6.2 Walk-In Participants

Walk-in participants have no prior communication. All engagement happens on-site.

| Touchpoint | Timing | Channel | Content |
|------------|--------|---------|---------|
| **Zone Signage** | Always visible | Physical / Digital display board | "Make Your Own Video Game with AI — 90 Minutes — Next Session: [TIME]" + QR for instant booking |

## 7. Emergency Protocols (Park-Specific)

| Emergency | Protocol |
|-----------|----------|
| Fire alarm | Follow park evacuation procedure. Instructor leads group to nearest exit. Do NOT attempt to secure equipment. |
| Medical emergency (participant) | Call park medical team immediately. Instructor stays with group, ops staff accompanies patient. |
| Power outage | Wait 5 minutes for park backup power. If not restored, end session early, issue "return visit" vouchers. |
| Network outage (LTE + park Wi-Fi both down) | Switch to offline template activity. Children draw game designs on paper. Issue partial refund or voucher. |
| Laptop theft | Report to park security immediately. File police report. Activate insurance claim. |
| Aggressive visitor | De-escalate calmly. If threatening, call park security. Do not engage physically. |
