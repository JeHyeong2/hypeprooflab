**$189 billion poured into startups in February alone.** A record-breaking month for venture capital. But [83% of that money went to just three companies](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/03/openai-anthropic-waymo-dominated-189-billion-vc-investments-february-crunchbase-report/).

OpenAI ($110B), Anthropic ($30B), and Waymo ($16B) captured nearly everything. Thousands of other startups had to fight over the remaining 17%.

## Investment Warning Signals

[BlackRock CEO Larry Fink issued warnings about AI investment](https://creati.ai/ai-news/2026-03-14/). He argued that accelerating AI investment competition will inevitably bankrupt companies that overleveraged on data centers and AI infrastructure.

Is this concentration sustainable? The fact that most of $189B flowed to just three companies raises questions about ecosystem health.

## GPT-5.4's Quiet Launch

[OpenAI quietly dropped GPT-5.4](https://llm-stats.com/llm-updates) with 1 million token context windows and autonomous workflow execution. [It scored 75% on OSWorld-V benchmarks](https://llm-stats.com/llm-updates), proving capable of real desktop productivity tasks.

Reactions are mixed. "You can now feed it the entire Harry Potter series and ask questions" versus "Real work gets done in 3-hour contexts, 1M tokens is just showing off."

[Anthropic also rolled out memory features to all Claude users](https://releasebot.io/updates/anthropic). Claude Sonnet 4.6 and Opus 4.6 offer 1 million token context window betas. The context war has begun.

## China's OpenClaw Concerns

Interestingly, [Chinese authorities issued guidelines restricting OpenClaw AI installations](https://article.wn.com/view/2026/03/13/Chinese_authorities_raise_concerns_over_OpenClaw_AI/) in state enterprises and government agencies. They recommended six security protocols: latest official versions only, minimal internet exposure, and least-privilege access.

[OpenClaw achieved over 210,000 GitHub stars, becoming the fastest-growing open source project in history](https://blog.bytebytego.com/p/top-ai-github-repositories-in-2026). This personal AI assistant connects with WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord and runs on your own hardware. Beijing's security concerns signal recognition of personal AI's political implications.

## Tomorrow's Watch

[Apple plans to relaunch AI-powered Siri with iOS 26.4 in March](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news). Google's 1.2 trillion parameter Gemini AI model will run on Apple Private Cloud Compute for privacy protection.

As BigTech's AI arms race intensifies, the real competition will be who seamlessly integrates into users' daily routines first.
