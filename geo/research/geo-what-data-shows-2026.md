# GEO: What the Data Actually Shows (2026 Research Summary)

> Source: PDF research report (saved as geo-what-data-shows-2026.pdf)

## Key Numbers

### Visibility Gains
- **30-40%** relative improvement (Position-Adjusted Word Count) from best GEO methods
- **115.1%** visibility increase for sites ranked 5th in traditional search (with inline citations)
- **Top-ranked sites decreased** — GEO inverts the Matthew Effect
- AutoGEO (CMU, Oct 2025): **50.99%** gains over original GEO baseline, avg 35.99%

### Best Methods (Princeton KDD 2024)
1. **Cite Sources** + **Statistics Addition** + **Quotation Addition** → 30-40% improvement
2. **Fluency + Statistics** combo → outperformed any single strategy by 5.5%+
3. **Keyword stuffing** → performed WORSE than unoptimized (especially on Perplexity)
4. Domain-specific: stats for law/govt, quotes for history, authoritative language for historical

### AI Traffic Reality
- AI platforms = **~1% of global web traffic** (Conductor 2026)
- ChatGPT drives **0.19%** of total traffic (Google sends 345× more)
- BUT **527% YoY growth** (first 5 months 2025)
- Adobe: **4,700% YoY surge** in AI-driven retail traffic by July 2025

### Conversion Rates (The Real Story)
- **SaaS/B2B**: AI traffic converts **23× better** (12.1% signups from 0.5% traffic)
- ChatGPT: **15.9%** conversion vs Google organic **1.76%**
- E-commerce: Initially lagged, but Holiday 2025 AI referrals converted **31% more**
- AI-referred visitors: **23% lower bounce**, **12% more page views**, **41% longer sessions**

### Citation Patterns by Platform
- Wikipedia = 7.8% of ChatGPT citations
- Reddit dominates Perplexity at 6.6%
- Only **11% of domains** cited by both ChatGPT AND Perplexity → platform-specific optimization required
- **76% of AI Overview citations** from Google top 10 pages
- **Only 12% of ChatGPT/Perplexity citations** rank in Google top 10
- **80% of ChatGPT citations** don't appear in Google's top 100

### Content Structure That Gets Cited
- Clear H2/H3 hierarchies + bullets → **40% more likely** to be cited
- Tables → **2.5× more citations**
- Long-form (2,000+ words) → **3× more citations**
- Listicles → **50% of top AI citations**
- Schema markup → **+36% citation probability**
- FAQ schema, Article schema, Product schema strongest
- Content freshness: AI-cited content **25.7% fresher** than organic results
- **76.4% of ChatGPT's most-cited pages** updated within 30 days

### AI Search Reliability Concerns
- Only **51.5%** of AI-generated sentences fully supported by citations (Stanford EMNLP 2023)
- ChatGPT Search: incorrect **67%** of the time
- Gemini: incorrect **81%**
- Grok-3: incorrect **94%**
- Perplexity: best at **37% failure rate**

### Market Impact
- AI Overviews on **25% of Google searches**
- CTR for Position #1 dropped from **7.3% to 2.6%** (-34.5%)
- Google AI Mode: **93% zero-click rate**
- 80% of consumers rely on zero-click results for 40%+ of searches
- GEO market: **$886 million**

## Tested Methods (Princeton, 9 total)
1. Keyword stuffing ❌ (worse than baseline)
2. Unique words
3. Technical terms
4. Authoritative language ✅ (good for historical)
5. Statistics addition ✅✅ (best for law/govt)
6. Quotation addition ✅✅ (best for history/people)
7. Cite sources ✅✅✅ (biggest impact, especially for lower-ranked sites)
8. Fluency optimization ✅✅
9. Easy-to-understand simplification

## Key Insight for HypeProof
- **Challengers > Incumbents** in GEO — perfect for us
- Inline citations to reputable sources = biggest lever
- Fresh content matters (update within 30 days)
- Schema markup (Article, FAQ) directly helps LLMs understand content
- Each AI platform has unique preferences → optimize per-platform
- Long-form, structured, evidence-based content wins → aligns with "증명한다" philosophy
